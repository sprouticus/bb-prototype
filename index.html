Why Now

1.5M more disabled workers have joined the U.S. workforce since 2020.

Employment among disabled individuals is at a record 22% and climbing — but most outdoor and physical jobs still don't have equipment built for them. The Enabler 2.1 is built to close that gap, one site at a time.
