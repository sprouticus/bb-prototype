Technical Data Sheet

Enabler 2.0 — Full Specs

Everything under the hood, the frame, and the lift, for the proven Enabler 2.0. When you reserve today, you're reserving the Enabler 2.1 — the next generation, built on everything shown here. Specs reflect the current introductory build and are subject to change as production scales.

01 Power

SystemAll-electric, 51.2V
BatteryLithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4), deep-cycle
Battery capacity150 Ah
Accessory battery1 × 12V
Motor torque110 ft-lb

02 Suspension & Driveline

Front suspensionIndependent, disc brakes
Rear axleStandard, with shocks and disc brakes
Gearbox2-speed — high / low, with reverse
SteeringPower-assist
Wheels5-lug mounting
Tires14" aggressive off-road

03 Weight & Dimensions

Weight2,000 lb
Length124"
Width67"
Height77.5"
Ground clearance8.25"
Wheelbase98"

04 Features

  • All-wheel drive
  • Rear, self-operated wheelchair lift
  • Multifunctional — remove the rear lift (four bolts, two wires) and it's ready for any driver
  • Removable front hood trunk for storage
  • Front and side cargo areas
  • Optional docking for manual or battery-powered wheelchairs
  • All-steel construction for rugged, extended use
  • Designed and produced in the U.S.A.
  • Optional dual folding rear seats for passengers
  • Standard mirrors, brake lights, turn signals, headlights
  • Rubber stability mat for wheelchair-based riders

05 Included Equipment

  • Optional standard driver's seat (swap from wheelchair-based operation)
  • 2,000 lb front winch
  • Mountable high-lift jack
  • Rear work lights
  • Split front windshield
  • Charging port for accessories & personal electronics
